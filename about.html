Hey! I'm Dani.

I enjoy building applications through a human-centered approach. I draw inspiration from fields in computational neuroscience, software algorithms, design thinking, and even oceanography. All the same, I am most excited about understanding the underlying architectures needed to build systems that can intelligently interact with the dynamic nature of humans, while also maintaining a focus on responsible, safe AI.

I created this portfolio to help me visualize the pieces of work I am proud of and the road I have taken to see them to completion. Even though some pieces may be found incomplete, this choice is intentional since I perceive learning as an evolutionary process and it illustrates my growth track.

Professionally, I am a machine learning, software engineer, and I'm driven to perfect my software craftsmanship. I studied computer science, biology, and stumbled into computational neuroscience during my undergrad. These years were also exciting for me because I conducted research in oceanography and published my first research paper as the first author. I later pursued a master's in computational neuroscience and machine learning.

I am open to collaborate with anyone who aligns with my interests and values. A well thought out message typically gets a reply from me.
